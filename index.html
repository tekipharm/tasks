SUMMARY

Great Isioma Oyita is a Pharmacist by profession and a health tech enthusiast. He worked as a volunteer with implementing partners IPs; Centre for Integrated Health Programme (CIHP) to support President's Emergency Plan for AIDS Relief (PEPFAR), USAID & GF funded health Programs at facilities as a Pharmacist for logistics and pharmaceutical care, supporting Gombe state Government in the area of Procurement & Supply Chain Management (PSM) for HIV & Maternal Newborn & Child Health (MNCH), Malaria as well as Infection control at the facility level. He was also privileged to be part of the hospital team on MTCT of HIV, assigned as focal Pharmacist to oversee the activities of the Society for Family Health (SFH) on Syndromic Management of Sexual Transmitted Diseases among targeted population (MSM, FSW, PWID and GP) in the Local Government Area. He currently works as a clinical pharmacist at Links and Ranks Pharmacy and as a Superintendent Telemedicine Pharmacist at Mobihealth International. Haven began a productive role in his professional career, Great Isioma Oyita's long term goal is to be able to raise young innovative leaders who have the passion for developing their immediate communities.
